Stefan Banach

The genius Polish mathematician

A short summary of Banach's life

  • 1892 - Born in Kraków, Austria-Hungary.
  • 1910 - Moves to Lwów to study at Lwów Polytechnic.
  • 1916 - Renowned professor Hugo Steinhaus discovers him and his friend Otto Nikody.
  • 1919 - With other mathematicians, he established Polish Mathematical Society.
  • 1920 - Thanks to Steinhaus' backing he receives doctorate without graduating.
  • 1922 - His doctoral thesis gets published and lays down the basics of functional analysis - entirely new branch of mathematics. This leads to him becoming a professor at the Lwów Polytechnic.
  • 1929 - Group gathered around him starts publishing journal - Studia Mathematica.
  • 1945 - He gets diagnosed with lung cancer and dies shortly after.
  • 1946 - Stefan Banach Prize gets established.
